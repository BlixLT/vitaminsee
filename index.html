vitaminsee by eglaysher

Image viewer for OSX

VitaminSEE is an image viewer for OSX that focuses on interface responsiveness and speed. It offers a clean interface and fast load times. VitaminSEE will automatically thumbnail your images, optionally saving them with the files. VitaminSEE has been tested on OS X, 10.4. I have gotten several bug reports about it freezing and crashing at startup under Leopard; I can not verify this because OSX is no longer my primary operating system, and I have no intention of buying Leopard.
