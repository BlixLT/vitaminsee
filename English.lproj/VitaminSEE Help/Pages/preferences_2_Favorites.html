Favorites Preferences

This pane allows you to add, reorder, and remove your favorite locations. These locations are used in the "Go > Favorite Locations" menu, the "Favorites" toolbar button, and the Sort Manager.

Clicking "Add" will bring up an Open Directory dialog where one or more directories can be selected. These directories will be added to the list of destinations.

Clicking remove will remove the selected destinations from the list.

Items can be renamed by double-clicking on their name.

By default, the only path listed is your Pictures folder.
